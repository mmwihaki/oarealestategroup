Meet Hunter

Hunter started his real estate career full steam ahead as he oversaw 24 sales and 40 leases totalling $40M during his time as Director of Operations for a top real estate broker in Los Angeles. The role allowed him to gain vast amounts of knowledge in all aspects of real estate from commercial, to probate, trust and income transactions. He quickly grasped a strong knowledge of the market and excelled at client relations.

Hunter prides himself most on his strong relationships with his clients. He goes far beyond most agents in his open and honest communication through all phases of the buying and selling process. His integrity, accessibility, honest assessment, attention to detail, organization and overall personal touch make clients feel valued and attended to. Hunter consistently and effectively advocates on behalf of his clients with their interests being his top priority. His skilled negotiation techniques constantly yield the best deals for his clients. He always makes time for each and every one of them and works tirelessly seven days a week to achieve their desired results.

Prior to real estate, Hunter started his career in Washington DC after graduating from the University of Southern California. He worked as a political producer for CNN in a variety of roles from Wolf Blitzer's show, to the White House and the 2012 election for which he won an Emmy Award. After seven years in Washington DC, he moved back to sunny California to the city he loves most, Los Angeles, to work at E! Entertainment covering celebrity news and pop culture before discovering his true passion for real estate. Hunter's love for exploring new properties, Los Angeles and helping people achieve their goals is why real estate is the perfect fit for him. Hunter is a member of the Beverly Hills/Greater Los Angeles Association of Realtors, the California Realtor Association, the National Association of Realtors.

Meet Austin

Austin began his career in real estate after working under one of the top producing agents in Los Angeles. This experience allowed him to gain the knowledge necessary to serve his clients at the highest level.

Being born and raised in California with a deep love for Los Angeles, he found a career in real estate to be the perfect fit. Austin seamlessly transitioned to real estate after previously working as a national and international male model and actor based in Los Angeles. The skills he learned in that industry helped him succeed at working with new clients, quickly adjusting based on their wants and needs to get a successful end goal.

Austin prides himself on putting his clients first and doing whatever it takes to have a smooth and rewarding transaction. No pressure, no sales, just honest and clear communication. His goal is to handle the entire transaction and to let his clients sit back and relax.

Austin is a part of the Beverly Hills Greater Los Angeles Association of Realtors, the California Association of Realtors and the National Association of Realtors.

description

The flagship development project by Open Air Real Estate. With the architecture completed by David Hertz and inspired by a one hundred year old avocado tree, this home has been freshly renovated from the ground up. With 3 bedrooms and 3 bathrooms just blocks from the ocean, this home is the model for California living.

Designed by Interior Particulier

Expansive yet homey, Washington Way was created with the best of both worlds in mind. Thanks to the blonde wood, textured natural stones, exposed rafters, and massive windows throughout, every part of this home glows with the warmth of natural light.

Designed to blend inside with outside, there’s ample space for lounging in the lush backyard. Whether you want to quietly digest a book with your favorite beverage or enjoy a family affair, there is room for a lively time.
